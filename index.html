How We're Capitalizing
The DR formalized online gaming in 2024 under Resolution 136-2024. With only 4 active operators and a licensing process of ~2 years, the market is effectively closed to new competitors until 2028. Appuesta's sportsbook license is in final approval stage, expected within 60 days.

At a $4M–$5M valuation, investors are buying into a company valued at just ~4% of the addressable market — a modest entry point for a licensed operator with established infrastructure.

We are positioned to capture early market share through:
  • LIDOM partnerships — stadium billboards, digital screens and path to jersey sponsorship, activating October 2026 for winter baseball league
  • Strategic entertainment partnerships — high-profile athletes and local sports teams onboard
  • AI-powered personalization — the platform analyzes each user's betting patterns to surface preferred sports, tailored odds and custom bet recommendations
  • Flexible payment infrastructure — CARDNET credit card processing and multiple bank deposit avenues

Traction & Proof Points
Appuesta launched in July 2025 and has been operating in Chile and Ecuador as a live proving ground. The platform has demonstrated real market demand:
  • $1M+ total turnover since July 2025 (10 months)
  • $43K+ total GGR generated with minimal marketing
  • 9,200+ registered users across two markets
  • 1.2M+ social media impressions since launch
  • 50–64% monthly retention in recent months
  • Player intelligence, CRM analytics, and bonus optimization tools built in-house
  • LIDOM partnership activating October 2026 for DR winter baseball league
